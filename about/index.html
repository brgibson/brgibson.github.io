About Ben

Born in Boston, MA, Ben moved to Southern Indiana with his mother and father at the age of four.

In 2010, he graduated from Indiana University with a double major in Mathematics and Telecommunications (Design and Production) and relocated to Chicago.

He currently lives in Southern California with his wife and works as a front-end web developer.
